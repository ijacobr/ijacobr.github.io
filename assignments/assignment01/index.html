League of Legends

League of Legends is a popular online multiplayer game developed by Riot Games. The game was released in 2009 and has since become one of the most popular games in the world. The game is a MOBA, or Multiplayer Online Battle Arena, where two teams of five players compete to destroy the enemy Nexus. The game is free to play, but players can purchase in-game items to customize their characters.

League of Legends has become so popular that Riot Games, the company who created LoL, has made lots of different games and media based on the League of Legends universe. One of the most popular games is Teamfight Tactics, a strategy game where players compete against each other in a battle of wits. Riot Games has also released a Netflix series called Arcane, which is based on the lore of League of Legends. Riot Games has already announced there will be a second season of Arcane.
